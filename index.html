About Lotto Pro

Lotto Pro is a lightweight tool for exploring random number picks. It focuses on clarity, a clean interface, and transparent behavior. You can pre-select up to six numbers, and the app fills the rest with random unique picks between 1 and 45.

How It Works

  1. Select up to six numbers from the grid.
  2. Click Generate to complete a six-number set.
  3. Click Clear to reset and try again.

Fairness & Responsible Use

This tool does not increase odds or guarantee outcomes. It provides random selections for entertainment and educational use. Always follow local laws and play responsibly.

FAQ

Are the numbers truly random?

The generator uses browser randomness to select unique numbers between 1 and 45.

Do you store my picks?

No. Picks remain in your browser and are cleared when you reset.

Can I save multiple sets?

Not yet. This version focuses on a fast single-set experience.
